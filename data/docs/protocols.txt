[Protocol: Handling Fragile Items]
Fragile items like bottles and glass must be handled with care.
Use the soft-grip effector. Max acceleration: 0.5 m/s.

[Protocol: Heavy Lifting]
Heavy items like suitcases require the hydraulic lift.
Ensure the path is clear. Do not lift over heads.

[Protocol: Hazardous Materials]
Hazardous items like electronics or sharp objects trigger a red warning.
Do not tilt more than 15 degrees.
